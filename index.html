Hi. I'm Abhishek Dey

About

I am currently working as a Senior AI Researcher at Kaliber.ai. I joined Kaliber Labs in August 2018 after completing my Masters. At Kaliber Labs, I work in the ML Researchers team who are involved in developing Computer Vision based AI Solutions for Endoscopic video feeds. Prior to that I was associated with the Speech Processing Group at the department of Electronics and Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati in the capacity of Assistant Project Engineer. I was mostly involved in developing Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) systems in native Indian languages like Assamese, Mizo etc in Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology (MEITY), Govt. of India sponsored R&D projects. I was also involved in developing IVR systems as a front end for interacting with end users and speech data collection. During my stay at IIT Guwahati, I was fortunate to work under the supervision of Prof. Rohit Sinha , Prof. S R M Prasanna , Prof. Priyankoo Sarmah and Prof. Samudravijaya K . I shall always be grateful to them for their support and guidance in shaping my career as an AI Researcher.
